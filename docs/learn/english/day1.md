### 5. 今日语法

**be动词的用法**：
- I am = I'm
- you are = you're
- he is = he's
- she is = she's
- it is = it's

**否定句**：
- I am not = I'm not
- you are not = you aren't

**疑问句**：
- Are you a student? Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
- Is this your bag? Yes, it is. / No, it isn't.
